Anodea Judith, Ph.D., has been called a prophet for our time—building bridges between the worlds of spirit and the practical work of healing our lives and our culture. For more than five decades, she has guided seekers, leaders, and change-makers through the intertwined paths of personal awakening and collective transformation, illuminating a way forward in times of uncertainty with clarity, compassion, and fierce love.

With academic training in psychology and religious studies, honed by lived experience, Judith has devoted her life to exploring the sacred architecture that connects mind, body, soul, and culture.

She is best known for her pioneering work with the chakra system, bringing this ancient map of consciousness into dialogue with Western psychology, somatic healing, yoga, manifestation, and cultural evolution. Through her work, the chakras became not only a system of personal growth, but a blueprint for transforming society itself.

From the love of power to the power of love

Her books—with well over a million copies in print in 29 languages—have become foundational texts for generations of students and teachers. Her teachings have circled the globe, reaching more than 160 nations through in person workshops, trainings, and online programs, touching countless lives with tools that are both practical and profound.

As a keynote speaker, Judith is known for presentations that feel less like lectures and more like journeys—cinematic, image-rich, and story-woven—inviting audiences to face the realities of our time with hope, and to see our current crises as evolutionary drivers rather than collapse.

Founder of Sacred Centers Academy, she now mentors a lineage of teachers carrying her work forward. She also collaborates with visionary networks such as Evolutionary Leaders Council, The Holomovement, Founding Mothers Movement, and her own She Rises Movement, all dedicated to shaping a more compassionate and life-affirming future.

Heaven down to earth through spiritual practice

At the heart of her message is a simple yet radical truth: humanity is passing through an initiatory threshold—a global rite of passage from the love of power to the power of love. Described in her award-winning book The Global Heart Awakens, she calls us to mature as a species, and to bring heaven down to earth through spiritual practice and conscious action.

Ordained as a Priestess in 1985, Judith has vowed to restore the cultural wisdom of the feminine, offering archetypal pathways for women's leadership, rekindling the fire of the Goddess, and working for a more balanced partnership society.

A global rite of passage for humanity

Now in her seventies, with the perspective of a lifetime devoted to service, her work carries the quiet authority of experience and the enduring optimism of one who has witnessed countless transformations. Her legacy is not only in the books she has written or the audiences she has reached, but in the thousands of people who have found their healing, their purpose, and awakened their gifts through her teachings.
